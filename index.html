How PDF Merger works

Combine any number of PDFs into one neat, ordered file. You control the page order — no quality loss, no watermarks.

1
Upload multiple PDFs — drag them in or click to browse from your device.
2
Reorder if needed — drag thumbnails up or down to set the page sequence.
3
Click Merge — all files are combined into one clean PDF instantly.
4
Download the result — your merged PDF is ready in seconds.

How PDF to Word works

Turn any PDF — including scanned documents — into a fully editable Word file. Our OCR engine extracts text accurately, keeping headings, tables, and paragraphs in place.

1
Upload your PDF — works with text-based and scanned PDFs.
2
OCR & extraction — our engine reads and structures your content automatically.
3
Review the output — check that tables, headings, and paragraphs are correct.
4
Download the .docx — edit it in Word, Google Docs, or any editor.
